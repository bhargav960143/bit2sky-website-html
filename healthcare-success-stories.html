About The Project

Epic Inc

Bit2Sky's collaboration with EPIC Inc., a non-profit organization dedicated to assisting individuals with disabilities, resulted in a transformative healthcare solution tailored to their unique needs. By addressing challenges such as outdated technology, complex processes, and compliance issues, Bit2Sky delivered a comprehensive Healthcare Solution Platform featuring a modernized Electronic Health Record system, integrated billing, and seamless data migration. With a commitment to client-centricity and innovative solutions, Bit2Sky empowered EPIC Inc. to streamline operations, improve service delivery, and fulfil its mission of enhancing the lives of individuals with disabilities.

About The Project

CSAAC Inc

In partnership with Bit2Sky, Community Services for Autistic Adults and Children (CSAAC) underwent a transformative journey, upgrading its healthcare infrastructure to streamline operations and enhance patient care. Bit2Sky's Healthcare Solution Platform integrated seamlessly with CSAAC's systems, overcoming the challenges of disconnected data and outdated technology. By leveraging Microsoft Azure and a human-centric approach, Bit2Sky delivered a user-friendly Electronic Health Record (EHR) solution, empowering CSAAC to provide personalized, community-based services to nearly 300 families affected by autism. With a obligation to innovation and client satisfaction, Bit2Sky's collaboration with CSAAC exemplifies a successful digital transformation in the healthcare sector.
